Encrypt & Decrypt Text Online

Online Encrypter and Decrypter. Insert your text and a numeric key and submit the form to crypt or decrypt a string.

Please note that this tool is intended for personal use and not for professional purposes. I used a jQuery script to make encryption and decryption of text strings easy and accessible. You can find it here. Feel free to utilize this resource to secure personal information, ensuring direct control over the security of your messages.
